Independent Analysis Verdict — March 2026
The 10K Laptops Project by Dr Ayilola Ayotomiwa is legitimate, transparent, and demonstrably impactful. ₦21.26M was raised, ₦14.04M deployed on 54 verified laptops, ₦3.9M invested in a free tech school with 400+ active students. Every accusation of misconduct examined in this analysis was debunked by publicly available video, data, and beneficiary testimony. The project's primary structural gap is not accountability — it is the absence of a single, permanent, live platform that makes the accountability undeniable without requiring Ayilola to defend himself manually on X.
